Now Under New Ownership

Justin Dotson — Owner

Frenzy INK has officially passed the torch. After years in the shop—earning the trust of the crew and the community—Justin Dotson is now the official owner of Frenzy INK.

Justin's been behind the scenes and in the chair long enough to know exactly what makes Frenzy, Frenzy. His plan is simple: honor everything Todd built, keep the quality climbing, and push the shop into its next era.

“I would like to take this opportunity to share with everyone — I have officially passed the torch to Justin Dotson. Justin is now the official owner of Frenzy INK. I am very proud of him and he has worked for and earned every bit of it. I trust that he will take Frenzy INK to the next level, and I hope everyone will show him the love and support he deserves.”
— Todd “Frenzy” Edwards, Founder
Our Story

Welcome to Frenzy INK

The Shop

Located in the heart of Johnson City, TN, Frenzy INK is the premier tattoo shop that has redefined the art of body ink. A welcoming atmosphere where creativity meets professionalism.

The Artists

Our skilled artists are renowned for diverse styles—traditional, neo-traditional, black & grey, color realism, fine line, and everything in between. Every client finds a tattoo that resonates.

The Standards

Cleanliness and safety come first. We adhere to the highest hygiene standards in the industry. Talented artists, passionate craft, consultations that actually listen.

The Aftercare

We offer top quality all-natural, all-vegan aftercare—not only for tattoo care but for all your skin care needs. A commitment to clients that extends far beyond the session.
